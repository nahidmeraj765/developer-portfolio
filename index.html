The Man Behind the Mask

Peter Parker is a regular high school student with a secret—he’s also Spider-Man. After being bitten by a radioactive spider, he gains incredible powers and uses them to protect New York City, guided by the belief that "with great power comes great responsibility." Despite the challenges, Peter is committed to doing what's right, balancing his life as a hero and a teenager.

Spider Man

The Hero Next Door

Spider-Man is a superhero with incredible agility, strength, and the ability to cling to walls and sense danger. After being bitten by a radioactive spider, he gains these powers and dedicates himself to protecting New York City from criminals and supervillains. Balancing his life as a high school student and a hero, Spider-Man is driven by the belief that "with great power comes great responsibility," always striving to do what's right despite the personal challenges he faces.
